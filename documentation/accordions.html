Accordions are modeled after Bootstrap accordions, with modifications for accessibility (including a larger hit-state, open/close icons, and right-to-left support).

For screan readers, role="heading" and aria-level="x" attributes are present on the span tag with class="title". In this way, we avoid nesting headers within links, which can confuse screan readers.

In the example below, Bootstrap's header class h4 is applied to the <a> tag, while <span class="title"> has role="heading" and aria-level="3" attributes for screen readers.
